Crane

Start with the colored side facing down. Fold the Paper in half horizontally and vertically.

Turn the paper over so the colored side is facing upwards. Fold in half diagonally.

Turn the paper to the side you started on and fold on all of your previous creases. This photo shows the step in progress.

The model should look like this when the previous step is completed.

Fold the edges to the center. Repeat on the other side.

Unfold the previous step and fold the top down like so.

Fold the flap up and repeat on the other side.

This is what it will look like when both flaps are folded up and flattened.

The legs that are hanging down need to be folded up. This is what it will look like.

The legs that you just folded in need to be spread out. Grab them and gently move them outward. Flatten.

Reverse fold the head on one side.

All Done!!!
