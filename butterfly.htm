Butterfly

Start with the color side facing up. Fold the paper in half in both directions

Turn the paper over so that the color side is facing down. Fold diagonally in both directions

Collapse the model (This is the collapse in progress)

Collapsed

Fold one flap on the left up to the top and repeat on the right

Turn the model over like so

Fold the bottom up a little past the top

Pull the two flaps down and flatten the model

Turn the model over and fold the tip down

Fold the tip back up a little

Fold the model in half

Fold to make the wings

Repeat step 12 on the other side

Done!
